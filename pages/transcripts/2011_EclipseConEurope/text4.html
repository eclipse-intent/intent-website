But enough with theoretical talk : Developer do need high-level doc, and it's not me who say it, but the developers themselves.

In a recent survey performed on a very large number of people, from open-source and Proprietary softwares, developers were asked what kind of doc they would want to use, and what kind of doc they actually use in their day-to-day work.

The results are stunning : a large number of developers would like to have high-level overviews of softwares and architecture, a lot more than Javadocs, which do not help to understand a software.

But one can ask why, if high-level doc is wanted that much, almost none of them actually use it, and have to read javadoc instead.
